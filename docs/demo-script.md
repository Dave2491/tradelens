# Demo Script

Use this as a short recording guide for the hackathon demo.

## 1. Open TradeLens

"This is TradeLens, an AI-assisted futures signal risk checker for Bitget AI Base Camp. It helps traders review crypto signals before following them."

## 2. Paste a signal

"I can paste a plain-English trade signal from a group chat or social feed. The app reads the pair, direction, entry, target, stop, and leverage."

Example:

```txt
SHORT HYPEUSDT
Entry: around current
TP: 60.85 / 59.62
SL: 62.71
Leverage: 2x
```

## 3. Show live Bitget checks

"TradeLens pulls live Bitget futures data. The verdict uses live price, volatility, trend, funding, order-book depth, and other market context."

Simple explanation:

- volatility means how much price is moving
- funding means the cost paid between long and short futures traders
- order-book depth shows visible buying and selling interest near current price

## 4. Show the AI review

"Qwen explains the trade in plain language. The AI does not invent the market data; it explains the risk facts TradeLens already calculated."

## 5. Show the comparison

"TradeLens compares the original signal against a safer version. This helps show whether the original trade needs a better stop, lower leverage, or a different target."

## 6. Paper-track both plans

"I can paper-track the original signal and the safer TradeLens plan side by side. This does not place real trades. It records what would have happened using Bitget market movement."

## 7. Open Evidence

"The Evidence page shows tracked trades, completed outcomes, net PnL, and whether TradeLens improved the result. The evidence can be exported as JSON for review."

## 8. Close

"TradeLens is not a trading bot and does not promise profit. It is a risk layer for futures traders who want to check signals before putting real money at risk."
